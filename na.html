WELCOME TO EN HOSPITALS

EN Hospital, Bangalore renders 360 degree advanced tertiary healthcare services with multi-super speciality and a state-of-the-art multi-organ transplant service. With the finest combination of experience, expertise, state-of-the-art technology and well-coordinated patient-centric teamwork, every step is aimed at ensuring excellence in patient care. The hospital strives to establish trust and excellence, following in the footsteps of the EN Group- a large conglomerate with a leading presence in education, medical, transport and infotainment. EN Hospital, Bangalore’s vision is to offer an advanced full range of primary and speciality care medical care services as well as enabling cross speciality consultation for unmatched patient experience. Institute of Cardiac Science, Institute of Neuroscience, Institute of Orthopaedics, Institute of Craniofacial, Aesthetics & Plastic Surgery, Institute of Gastroenterology, Hepatobiliary Sciences & Liver Transplant, Institute of Obstetrics, Gynaecology & IVF, Institute of Oncology, Institute of Anaesthesiology & Pain Services, Institute of Renal Science & Transplantation, OTHER MAJOR SPECIALTIES, ENT, Critical Care Unit, Chest Medicine, Emergency Medical Services, Internal Medicine, General Surgery, Pediatrics, Pediatric Intensive Care, Pediatric Surgery & Pediatric Urology, Neonatology, Diabetology, Infectious Disease, Psychiatry and Endocrinology,are teh some of the services provided by us.

EN Hospital, Bangalore is one of the fastest growing multi-super speciality tertiary care hospitals in India, spread over 2,30,000 sq ft. The patient-friendly facilities at EN Hospital, Bangalore will ensure to deliver the exceptional quality of patient care. The facilities are designed and equipped to meet the discerning requirements of both domestic and international patients. The patient rooms are designed with all modern comforts. Patients and family members experience a serene environment that offers them privacy and space, giving them a new experience in the health care industry.The International Patient Services at EN Hospital, Bangalore ensure to strike the chord of care in every patient from the moment they establish contact till the time they are discharged after treatment and cure. Customized cuisine suiting every palate, language translators, dedicated relationship managers, concierge services and Specialists who go the extra mile to comfort the patients & family members with the assurance of care and treatment, accommodation facilities to suit all budgets and other such initiatives have all ensure delivery of world-class treatment possible. As a health care provider EN Hospital, Bangalore will continue making a difference in everyone’s life and will take the spirit of leadership well beyond business metrics.
